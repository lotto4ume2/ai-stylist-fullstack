# AI Stylist - User Guide

Welcome to your AI-powered digital closet! This guide will walk you through all the features of your new application.

## 1. Getting Started

### Signing Up
1.  Navigate to the signup page.
2.  Enter your email and a secure password.
3.  Click **Sign Up** to create your account.

### Logging In
1.  Go to the login page.
2.  Enter your credentials.
3.  You will be redirected to your digital closet.

## 2. Your Digital Closet

### Uploading Items
-   **Drag and Drop**: Simply drag an image of your clothing item onto the upload area.
-   **Click to Upload**: Click the upload area to open a file browser and select your image.
-   Add details like **category**, **color**, **brand**, and **notes** to better organize your closet.

### Viewing Your Closet
-   **Grid View**: See a beautiful grid of all your clothing items.
-   **List View**: Switch to a more detailed list format.
-   **Favorites**: Mark items with a ❤️ to easily find them later.

### Filtering and Searching
-   **Search Bar**: Quickly find items by typing keywords.
-   **Filters**: Narrow down your closet by **category**, **color**, or **favorites**.

## 3. AI-Powered Features

### Outfit Recommendations
-   Navigate to the **Recommendations** page.
-   Get 3 complete outfit suggestions based on your current closet items.
-   Provide context like **occasion** or **weather** for more tailored suggestions.

### Closet Analysis
-   Curious about what your wardrobe is missing?
-   Use the **Closet Analysis** feature to get AI-powered suggestions for essential items to add.

### Weather-Based Suggestions
-   Get outfit ideas based on the current weather in your location.

## 4. Social and Planning

### Outfit Planner
-   Plan your outfits for the week ahead.
-   Select items, name your outfit, and assign it to a specific date.

### Share Your Style
-   Create a shareable link for any outfit.
-   Send it to friends or post it on social media.

### Inspiration
-   Feeling uninspired? The **Inspiration** page generates random outfit ideas from your closet to spark your creativity.

## 5. Settings

### Dark Mode
-   Switch between light and dark themes for a comfortable viewing experience at any time of day.

Enjoy your new AI Stylist!
